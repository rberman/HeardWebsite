How it Works

Review

Check out the map of locations where the herd has reported sexism. Our interactive map is color coded, and you can click on tags for more information about the incident.

Report

Experience sexism? It's incredibly easy to report an incident of sexism that you experience, and that report can help others avoid dangerous areas and see sexism in their communities.

Support

Read a comment that you empathize with, or have advice for a fellow Hearder? Comment on reports and join our supportive community of women who will no longer ignore everyday sexism.

About Us

Heard maps instances of verbal, physical, and systemic sexism to raise awareness of the harsh realities that women face every day. By recording instances of sexism that go ignored and unreported, we aim to change attitudes towards sexism. Where women once ignored or silently accepted sexism, now they can stand up and speak out.
